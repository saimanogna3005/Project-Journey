Kanyakumari Vivekananda Rock

9.5 10 ratings form Rs.500 Rock

The memorial hosts a light and sound show that narrates the life and teachings of Swami Vivekananda.It's also a popular spot for watching the sunrise and sunset.

Thanjavur Brihadeeswarar Temple

9.7 10 ratings form Rs.400 Temple

The Brihadeeswarar Temple is not only an architectural marvel but also an important pilgrimage site for Hindus and a cultural treasure that attracts tourists and history enthusiasts from around the world.

Rameswaram Pamban Rail Bridge

9.8 10 ratings form Rs.800 Rail Bridge

The Pamban Bridge has become a popular tourist attraction. Many tourists visit Rameswaram to experience the train journey across the bridge and enjoy the scenic beauty of the area.

Kodaikanal Hill Station

9.9 10 ratings form Rs.450 Hill Station

Some visitors come to Kodaikanal for its peaceful and tranquil environment, making it a great place for yoga and meditation retreats.You can explore the natural beauty on various trails.

Vellore Fort

9.4 10 ratings form Rs.250 Fort

Vellore Fort is not only a historical treasure but also a cultural and architectural marvel that showcases the heritage of the region. It is a must-visit destination for history enthusiasts and tourists exploring the southern part of India.

Chennai Marina Beach

9.4 10 ratings form Rs.250 Beach

The beach is known for its picturesque views of the sea, with golden sands and a beautiful shoreline. It's a popular spot for locals and tourists to relax, enjoy the sea breeze, and watch the sunset.

Hogenakkal Falls

9.5 10 ratings form Rs.500 Falls

It is often referred to as the "Niagara Falls of India," Hogenakkal is a popular tourist destination known for its stunning natural beauty and its therapeutic and medicinal properties.

Mudumalai National Park
